Heart Disease

Description

Heart Disease (cardiovascular disease) refers to conditions that involve narrowed or blocked blood vessels that can lead to a heart attack, chest pain (angina) or stroke.

Other heart conditions, such as those that affect your heart's muscle, valves or rhythm, also are considered forms of heart disease.

Symptoms

Chest Pain, Tightness and Pressure
Chest Discomfort (angina)
Shortness of Breath
Weakness or Coldness in Legs or Arms
Pain in the Neck and Back
Numbness
Pain in the Jaw and Throat

Precautions

Control your Blood Pressure
Eat a Healthy Diet
Limit Alcohol and Do not Smoke
Get Regular Exercise
Keep Cholesterol levels under control
Manage Stress
Manage Diabetes
